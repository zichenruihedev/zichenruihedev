Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services

What types of apps do you develop?

We specialize in productivity and wellness applications including focus timers, habit trackers, note-taking tools, decision-making assistants, mood trackers, and device wellness apps for both iOS and Android platforms.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple app typically takes 2-3 months, while more complex applications can take 4-6 months or longer. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Yes, we provide comprehensive post-launch support including bug fixes, performance optimization, regular updates, and feature enhancements. We also offer ongoing maintenance packages tailored to your needs.
